## Strategies This Pass (ranked by confidence)

### 1. Gold/Bitcoin Dual Momentum — 49 (Experimental)

- **Asset class:** Alternatives (Gold + Crypto)
- **Timeframe:** Weekly (rebalance every Wednesday)
- **Core concept:** Apply Antonacci's dual momentum (relative + absolute) framework to GLD
  and IBIT ETFs. Allocate weekly to whichever "store of value" asset has superior X-week
  momentum and positive absolute momentum; cash otherwise. A 20% vol cap via 12-week rolling
  standard deviation constrains position size.
- **Evidence status:** `CLAIMED, UNVERIFIED` — Quantpedia blog (Dujava, April 2026); not
  peer-reviewed; no open code.
- **Source links:** https://quantpedia.com/dual-momentum-allocation-between-physical-gold-and-bitcoin-digital-gold/
- **Verification level:** Low — Quantpedia is reputable but this is a blog post, not a
  peer-reviewed study. 10 lookback variants tested without OOS testing.
- **Analyst note:** The vol-capped composite (12.01% CAGR, Sharpe 1.37, max DD -12.27%)
  is the only version worth taking seriously. The uncapped 79.91% CAGR is driven by
  Bitcoin's structural bull run and lookback data-mining, not a robust systematic edge.
  The conceptual mechanism is plausible but the evidence base is too thin to advance.
