## Strategies This Pass (ranked by confidence)

### 1. Gold/Bitcoin Dual Momentum — 49 (Experimental)

- **Asset class:** Alternatives (Gold + Crypto)
- **Timeframe:** Weekly (rebalance every Wednesday)
- **Core concept:** Apply Antonacci's dual momentum (relative + absolute) framework to GLD
  and IBIT ETFs. Allocate weekly to whichever "store of value" asset has superior X-week
  momentum and positive absolute momentum; cash otherwise. A 20% vol cap via 12-week rolling
  standard deviation constrains position size.
- **Evidence status:** `CLAIMED, UNVERIFIED` — Quantpedia blog (Dujava, April 2026); not
  peer-reviewed; no open code.
- **Source links:** https://quantpedia.com/dual-momentum-allocation-between-physical-gold-and-bitcoin-digital-gold/
- **Verification level:** Low — Quantpedia is reputable but this is a blog post, not a
  peer-reviewed study. 10 lookback variants tested without OOS testing.
- **Analyst note:** The vol-capped composite (12.01% CAGR, Sharpe 1.37, max DD -12.27%)
  is the only version worth taking seriously. The uncapped 79.91% CAGR is driven by
  Bitcoin's structural bull run and lookback data-mining, not a robust systematic edge.
  The conceptual mechanism is plausible but the evidence base is too thin to advance.

### 2. TSMOM in Chinese Commodity Futures — 60 (Worth Researching)

- **Asset class:** Chinese Commodity Futures (SHFE + DCE + CZCE)
- **Timeframe:** Monthly (1-month lookback + 1-month hold)
- **Core concept:** Sign of past 1-month return determines long/short position in each of 64
  Chinese commodity futures. Volatility-managed position sizing. Four enhanced strategies also
  developed. Peer-reviewed in Journal of Futures Markets (Sept 2025).
- **Evidence status:** `CLAIMED, UNVERIFIED` for specific metrics (paywalled); the general
  methodology is reproducible from published literature. Multiple peer-reviewed papers confirm.
- **Source links:** Zheng et al. JFoM 2025 (DOI: 10.1002/fut.70033); Cho et al. JFoM 2019;
  Liu et al. Economic Modelling 2023
- **Verification level:** Moderate — peer-reviewed, 20-year dataset, multiple confirming
  papers; but specific Sharpe/drawdown are paywalled and the basic Sharpe 0.07 (from related
  paper) is thin.
- **Analyst note:** Strategy is effectively China-only (accessibility constraint is critical).
  The enhanced strategies need paper access. The TSMOM mechanism is well-justified; the
  regulatory intervention risk is the key unique concern for Chinese markets.

### 3. TrendFolios® Fixed Income Strategy — 40 (Experimental)

- **Asset class:** Fixed Income ETFs (inflation, duration, credit, currency)
- **Timeframe:** Bi-weekly rebalancing
- **Core concept:** Combine "line-based momentum" and "curve-based trend" signals on a
  diversified fixed income ETF universe; size inversely to volatility; benchmark: Bloomberg
  US Agg. Claims 5.89%/yr excess returns since inception (22+ years).
- **Evidence status:** `CLAIMED, UNVERIFIED` — arXiv preprint; no peer review; proprietary
  signals; commercial strategy with registered trademark.
- **Source links:** arXiv 2506.09330 (https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.09330)
- **Verification level:** Low — arXiv preprint with proprietary signals; the 3-year Sharpe
  of 0.04 during the 2022 rate shock is the most informative and troubling data point.
- **Analyst note:** The strategy presents as a commercial pitch on arXiv. "Line-based" and
  "curve-based" are proprietary terms preventing replication. The 17–19% annualized volatility
  is inconsistent with a pure fixed income strategy (Bloomberg US Agg ~5% vol), suggesting
  either leverage, high-yield/EM exposure, or composite multi-asset reporting. The 2022 stress
  test (rate shock) produced near-zero performance, undermining the core value proposition.

## Emerging Patterns

- **Dual momentum / paired-switching** applied to novel asset pairs (Gold/Bitcoin, Gold/IEF)
  is a recurring theme in practitioner research. The mechanism is well-understood but
  back-test quality varies widely; most applications lack OOS validation.
- **Vol-cap overlays on high-volatility assets (especially crypto):** Multiple strategies
  (gold-bitcoin-dual-momentum, crypto-ts-xs-momentum-realistic, adaptivetrend-crypto)
  rely on realized volatility scaling. This consistently improves Sharpe but dramatically
  reduces absolute returns on crypto strategies.

  pages are all returning HTTP 403. Effective research this run required using web search
  summaries as the primary information source. This is a recurring challenge — consider
  alternative data access strategies for future runs.
